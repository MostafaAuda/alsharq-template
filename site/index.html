About Us

Your life deserve

An Egyptian company established in 2005 as a sole proprietorship ”New Concept”. Then, it has been updated in 2017 to “El-sharq For Urban Development” as a shareholding company consisting of 18 contributors with full paid capital 50 million EGP. By the executive director “Eng. Mohamed Omara.”

Projects

Arqa 62

A ready to move project that is distinguished by its great locations, entertainment and commercial area. (Waterway 3 and Centro Mall)

Location

Few minutes from the clubs area.
Few minutes from 90’s North and the South axis.
Few minutes from Mohamed Ibn Zayed axis

Building History

In 2012, the land was bought. Then in 2015 and 2016, the land was licensed and selling units began. By 2018, the primary delivery started. Finally in 2020, full delivery for the apartments and over 75% of the building are sold out.
